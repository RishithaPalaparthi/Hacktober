Posted by Rishitha P on October 1, 2020

What do you do when you get an assignment? Apart from procrastinating, we all do 1 other common thing.
We GOOGLEEEE ITT!!

Gaints like Google, Tesla and Apple work with over millions lines of code. It's not possible for a few individuals to build something of that scale.

On the other hand, Android has < 1600 employees; Whereas linux, the os, has just 1 employee with his garage as it's headquarters.

How did this work out?

The answer is.... All of them tuned towards OPEN SOURCE.

Ok wait..So, now What exactly open source?

As the name suggests, open source is letting the source code of a project be open to everyone. Well, HOW DOES THIS HELPP? Many developers like us, who love freeware contribute towards its development.

Not satisfied? We aren't either.

Let's have a look at a few established open source projects that we all know:


*ANDROID* *CHROMIUM* *PYTHON* *FIREFOX*


Hacktober?? What does that have to do with this?

Hacktoberfest is a global anuual event that celebrates open source throughout the month of october.
It is open to everyone, irrespective of your occupation/role, proficiency with a language or coding as a whole.

"The power of open source is people. People rule."

This year(2020), if you make 4 or more valid pull requests you can either choose to get a tee or plant a tree in the name of open source.

PRs(Pull Requests) can be made to any public repo on GitHub, not only the ones with issues labeled hacktoberfest

Why we love it.

Networking with like minded people from all over the world, many fun events with goodies, T-shirts or even planting a tree, all of this for *FREE*.

We get to test and learn about new technologies that make our lives easier. And it goes without saying, open source would be the perfect icing on your resume.

To go places and do things that have never been done before – that’s what living is all about.
